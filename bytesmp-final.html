About

We are an Economy SMP

ByteSMP is a no-claims economy server. No protection, no hand-holding — just you, your skills, and the community.

Economy meets freedom

There are no protected areas, no anti-grief plugins, and no admin help for lost items. Build, steal, blow things up, and trade — everything is allowed as long as you face the consequences.

Here are the rules of the server. Keep them in mind, and you'll have a great time.

Rules

Here are the rules of the server

We keep it minimal. What is not forbidden is allowed. These rules apply to the Minecraft server, Discord, and all connected services.

§1

General Rules

  • By joining ByteSMP, you accept all rules in this document.
  • Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse.
  • Violations result in appropriate punishments; repeat offenses escalate.
  • These rules apply to: Minecraft server (ByteSMP.de), Discord, website, and all connected services.
  • For questions, open a ticket in our support system.
§2

Code of Conduct

  • Respect Everyone — Treat players and staff with basic respect. Toxicity isn't welcome.
  • No Discrimination — Racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, religious hate, or any slur is strictly forbidden.
  • No Harassment — Bullying, doxxing, or targeting specific players is prohibited.
  • No Spamming — Don't flood chat, voice, or DMs.
  • No Ghostpinging — Pinging and deleting to annoy is not allowed.
  • No Misinformation — Spreading harmful or malicious false info is banned.
  • No Drama — Keep personal beef out of public channels; use DMs or tickets.
  • Common Sense — If you're ruining the experience for others in an extreme way, staff may step in even if not explicitly listed.
§3

Advertising

  • Advertising is allowed only in designated channels.
  • Advertising other Minecraft servers, Discord servers, or external services is strictly forbidden.
  • Unsolicited ad DMs are also prohibited.
§4

Modifications & Clients

  • ALLOWED Badlion, Lunar Client, Vanilla, Feather (standard), Optifine, Sodium, QoL mods.
  • GRAY ZONE — Ask staff before using: minimaps with built-in cave/entity radar (as part of an allowed client), Freecam (screenshots only), macros (not fully automated).
  • BANNED Unknown/unverified clients, X-Ray, Autoclicker, Killaura, Fly/Speed/NoFall, Freecam (for PvP/scouting), Cave finder, Entity radar (standalone), Auto-totem, Duplication mods, any client that bypasses server mechanics.
  • If you're unsure, ask staff before logging in with it.
§5

Bugs & Glitches

  • No Exploiting — Using bugs, glitches, or unintended mechanics for personal gain is banned.
  • Report Bugs — Found a bug? Report it via ticket immediately — don't share it publicly.
  • Minor Issues — Even small visual/text bugs should be reported.
  • No Teaching Exploits — Sharing exploit methods with others is punishable.
  • Benefiting from Known Bugs — If you abuse a known issue, you may face rollbacks or bans.
§6

Staff Regulations

  • Follow Staff Instructions — Reasonable staff requests must be followed.
  • Respect Staff Decisions — Staff have final say, even without showing proof (though they usually will).
  • No Staff Harassment — Don't spam, insult, or be rude to staff.
  • Report Staff Abuse — If a staff member misbehaves, report them to a higher-up via ticket.
  • No Asking for Private Info — Staff will NEVER ask for your password, token, or IP. Report anyone who does.
§7

Support Rules

  • Use Tickets Properly — Only for real issues. No joke tickets.
  • No Fake Reports — Falsely accusing someone is prohibited.
  • No Staff Hunting — Don't try to find hidden staff.
  • No Troll Requests — Wasting staff time is punishable.
  • No Staff Impersonation — Pretending to be staff is forbidden.
  • No Staff Blocking — Hindering staff from doing their job will be punished.
§8

Accounts & Multiple Use

  • In-Game Alts — Multiple accounts are allowed.
  • Discord Accounts — One Discord account per person only.
  • Compromised Accounts — If your account is hacked, report it immediately via ticket.
  • Account Sharing — You are responsible for anything done on your account.
  • Ban Evasion — Using an alt to evade a ban will extend the ban to ALL your accounts.
§9

Privacy & Security

  • No Sharing Personal Info — Full name, address, IP, phone number, email, passwords, tokens, or any other PII is strictly forbidden.
  • Private Sharing Only — You may share your own contact info in private with express permission. Never post sensitive data in public channels.
  • Report Doxxing — If someone shares your info, tell staff immediately.
  • Stay Safe — Staff will never ask for your password.
§10

Rule Changes & Updates

  • Rules May Change — We can update these at any time.
  • No Advance Notice Required — Changes can happen without warning.
  • Stay Updated — It's your responsibility to keep up.
  • No Leaks — Leaking unreleased updates or features is forbidden.
§11

Offensive Content

  • Sexism & misogyny
  • Racism & xenophobia
  • Homophobia & transphobia
  • Religious hatred
  • Excessive targeted insults & profanity
  • Glorification of violence, drugs, or self-harm
  • Pornography, NSFW content, or sexual material
  • Gore or extreme violence
§12

Building & Griefing

  • Griefing is ALLOWED — Destroy, steal, and raid to your heart's content. That's the nature of the server.
  • No Offensive Builds — Buildings with offensive content (§11) are prohibited. Staff will remove them.
  • Screenshots Before Removal — If staff remove an offensive build, a screenshot will be taken for documentation.
  • No Lag Machines — Redstone contraptions or farms that intentionally cause severe server lag are banned.
  • No Exploit Building — Building using bugs, exploits, or unintended mechanics is not allowed.
  • No Spawn Griefing — Griefing the spawn area (where new players appear) is not allowed — keep it livable.
Yes, you can raid bases, steal items, and blow things up. That's the experience!
§13

Suggestions

  • No Duplicates — Duplicate suggestions will be rejected.
  • No Offensive Suggestions — Any suggestion violating §11 will be denied.
  • Be Constructive — Explain your idea clearly and why it would help.
  • Clear Title — Use a descriptive title.
§14

Unban Requests

  • No Spam — Don't spam unban requests.
  • 1-Month Wait — If your request is rejected, wait 1 month before trying again.
  • Ignoring the Wait — Attempting to bypass the wait may get you permanently excluded.
  • Be Honest — Lying in your request almost guarantees rejection.
§15

Punishments

  • Verbal warning
  • Chat mute / timeout
  • Temporary kick
  • Temporary ban (1–30 days)
  • Permanent ban
  • Discord ban
  • Event ban
  • Inventory wipe / rollback
  • Economic reset
Staff have discretion to choose punishments they find fair.
§16

Economy & Trading

  • Scamming IS ALLOWED — You are free to scam other players. Buyer beware! However, if you scam excessively to the point of destroying the community, staff may step in.
  • NO Real-Money Trading (RMT) — Trading in-game items, currency, or services for real-world money is strictly forbidden. No exceptions.
  • No RMT Discussion — Advertising, discussing, or facilitating RMT is prohibited.
  • No Item Duplication — Duplicating items via bugs or exploits is banned. We will catch you.
  • No Market Exploits — Manipulating the economy through unintended mechanics is not allowed.
  • Trash Talk Is Allowed — Competitive banter is fine, as long as it doesn't cross into harassment or discrimination.
  • No Trade Stealing — Interfering with an active trade to steal items mid-exchange is prohibited (scam before or after, not during).
§17

Voice Chat Rules

  • No soundboards without permission
  • No loud / annoying noises
  • Keep background noise reasonable (use push-to-talk if needed)
  • Be respectful — same conduct rules as text
  • No mic spam
  • No toxicity, yelling, or insults
  • No impersonation
  • No NSFW audio
  • Comply with staff if asked to mute or lower volume
